Preaching the Doctrines of Grace

Pilgrim Bible Church is a family-friendly Reformed Baptist church located in the Tacoma, Washington, area. We are easily accessible from Gig Harbor and the Peninsula.

We seek to exalt Christ through the preaching and teaching of God’s Word, prayer, singing, fellowship, and evangelism. We would love to have you join us for Sunday morning worship!
